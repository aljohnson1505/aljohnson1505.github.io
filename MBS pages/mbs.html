Mind Body Spirit

This series was a way for me to explore and represent the way that I understand my mind, body, and soul. Each image I chose was an image that I found to be personally relavant and significant. However I made an intentional effort to present those images so that they are approachable to an outside veiwer through the emotional implications they create. These panels were designed to feel interconected but distinct, since that is how I conceptualize the concepts of body, mind, and soul. You can read about my design process here.
